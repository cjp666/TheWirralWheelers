Welcome to the Wirral Wheelers we are an affiliated club part of the national network of the Cycling UK

We run rides starting mainly on the Wirral Peninsular & Chester. There are rides every Sunday, Impromptu Wednesday rides, and monthly Saturday rides. We organise full day cycle rides take a look at our rides to see where we are going and see if any appeal to you. With rides into the lanes of Wirral, Cheshire, and the hillier parts of North Wales

We want as many people as possible to enjoy cycling. We are a friendly group - why not come along

We cycle mostly on surfaced roads at an average speed of 10-12 miles per hour - but speeds and surfaces do alter all you need is a normal road bike to ride with us. We arrange routes with plenty of sight-seeing, eating and drinking. Our rides are led by experienced runs leaders
